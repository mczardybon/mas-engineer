## 3. Re-Engagement Campaign (4 Emails — Lapsed Users)

**Trigger**: No login or automation activity for 60+ days

### Email 1 — "We Miss You" (Soft Touch)
- **Subject**: "Haven't seen you — what's been keeping you busy?"
- **Send time**: Day 60 of inactivity, Tuesday 10:00 AM EST
- **Body outline**:
  - Friendly, non-salesy check-in
  - "We've made some improvements since your last visit"
  - 1-bullet list of 3 new features (simplified)
  - Low-friction CTA: just log back in
- **CTA**: "See What's New →"
- **Tone**: Curious, helpful — not pushy

### Email 2 — Value-Add (Use Case Reminder)
- **Subject**: "Remember why you signed up — here's a fresh idea"
- **Send time**: Day 68, Wednesday 11:00 AM EST
- **Body outline**:
  - Reframe the original problem they wanted to solve
  - One new use case they may not have tried
  - Quick-start template: "Copy this workflow in 2 clicks"
  - Testimonial from similar business owner
- **CTA**: "Try This Workflow →"
- **Note**: Segment by original sign-up industry if data available

### Email 3 — Offer & Urgency
- **Subject**: "Come back — 1 month free + priority support"
- **Send time**: Day 75, Thursday 10:30 AM EST
- **Body outline**:
  - Clear, valuable re-engagement offer
  - "1 month free on your next billing cycle"
  - Priority support access for 30 days
  - Simple steps to reactivate (3 bullets max)
  - Expiration date: 14 days
- **CTA**: "Claim Your Free Month →"
- **Scarcity**: "Offer ends [date]"

### Email 4 — Final Breakup (Win-Back or Farewell)
- **Subject**: "Should we pause your account?"
- **Send time**: Day 85, Tuesday 11:00 AM EST
- **Body outline**:
  - Respectful: "If things have changed, we understand"
  - Option A: Pause account (keep data, no charges)
  - Option B: Download data export (their content, their ownership)
  - Option C: One-click reactivation (if still interested)
  - Final feedback request: "Why did you leave?" (1-click survey)
- **CTA**: "Choose What's Best for You →"
- **GDPR note**: Data deletion option clearly linked
- **Tone**: Gracious, customer-first

## Best Practices Applied

| Practice | Implementation |
|---|---|
| **Send timing** | B2B SaaS audience → Tue-Thu, 10-11 AM EST |
| **Subject lines** | All under 55 chars, curiosity/personalization |
| **Body length** | 100-200 words, scannable with bullets |
| **CTA clarity** | Single action per email, button-style language |
| **Personalization** | Dynamic fields: name, hours saved, automations active |
| **Compliance** | Unsubscribe in every email, GDPR data mention for EU |
| **Spam avoidance** | Minimal caps, no trigger words (free, guaranteed, etc.) |
| **Mobile-friendly** | Short paragraphs, large CTAs |
